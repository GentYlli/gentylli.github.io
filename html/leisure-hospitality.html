    Ceilings for Leisure & Hospitality Buildings

    Tasteful Comfort from Sports to Supper

    When designing ceilings for leisure and hospitality venues, the priority is to create the perfect atmosphere for guests to relax. This means ensuring acoustic balance to optimise speech intelligibility, maximising natural light reflectance to lift moods, and managing humidity to ensure comfort. With our wide range of multi-material ceiling and wall solutions, shapes, and finishes, you’ll have complete freedom to design bespoke spaces that elevate guests’ experience.

    Benefits of Our Ceilings for Leisure Venues
    Durable aesthetics From high-end restaurants to high-tech gyms, our unique multi-material range allows you to create tailor-made ceiling designs for any leisure venue. These busy locations benefit greatly from our ceilings’ fire-, impact-, and scratch-resistant performance.
    Comfortable acoustics Balanced acoustic environments enhance occupant comfort, aid speech intelligibility, and support privacy. Our ceiling materials offer high sound absorption for clear communication and optimal sound attenuation to reduce distractions between rooms.
    Humidity resistance Humidity can cause mould or mildew with harmful consequences for occupants and costly damage to interiors. Our materials are suitable for installation in conditions of up to 95% relative humidity and up to 100% relative humidity for extreme environments.
    Restaurants & Cafeterias

    In dining areas, indulge guests with sophisticated restaurant ceiling designs that offer enhanced acoustics and an array of options to create the perfect setting. Back in the kitchen, our durable, cleanable ceiling materials help you meet the food industry’s highest hygiene standards.

    Lobbies & Reception Areas

    Make a grand entrance with mineral, metal, wood wool, or wood design ceilings in stylish shapes, colours, and finishes. Our materials offer balanced acoustics, so guests feel welcomed. Thanks to their durability, our ceilings ensure a lasting impression.

    Sports Halls

    Gymnasiums often have hard, reflective surfaces that can make the environment very noisy. Our ball impact resistant ceiling systems not only ensure balanced acoustics, but also keep gyms looking fit for years.

    Conference Rooms & Ballrooms

    Whether for wedding receptions or corporate meetings, our acoustically optimised ceilings and walls help conversations flow easier. Our unparalleled portfolio allows you to design elegant spaces for collaboration and celebration.
