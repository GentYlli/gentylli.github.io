    Ceilings for transportation buildings

    Safe and sound in a fast-paced world

    There are high expectations of ceilings in transportation buildings. Materials must be functional, maintainable, durable, and fire resistant. Our premium ceiling solutions fulfil all these criteria. They also allow you to create airports and train stations that are welcoming and calming. This is not only thanks to their optimal acoustic properties, but also the vast variety of materials, shapes, and colours we offer. Our global teams support you with expertise in local building regulations to help you fast-track your designs.

    Benefits of our ceilings for airports & train stations
    Secure fire resistance Materials used in transport hubs, like airports and train stations, are required to be fire resistant to ensure public safety. Our ceiling solutions are engineered to meet the most stringent safety standards and local regulations.
    Comfortable acoustics Balanced acoustic environments enhance occupant comfort, aid speech intelligibility, and support privacy. Our ceiling materials offer high sound absorption for clear communication and optimal sound attenuation to reduce distractions between rooms.
    High light reflectance Natural light has a positive effect on human mood and productivity. Returning up to 88% of light (versus 75% with standard ceilings), our ceiling tiles enhance occupant wellbeing and reduce the need for artificial lighting, thus also saving energy.
    Main concourse

    Our ceiling and wall system solutions absorb noise to keep travellers calm in high-traffic areas. With our extensive designs and technical expertise, we help you create ceilings that flow with the curves of a concourse and provide full downward accessibility for maintenance.

    Waiting & departure areas

    Passengers need spaces to comfortably eat, sleep, work, and play while waiting for connections. Our ceilings allow you to design welcoming, quiet areas with superior acoustical performance.

    Passenger lounges

    Create first-class elegance and comfort in lounges with our metal and wood design ceilings, available in a variety of shapes, colours, and designs.

    Restaurants & food courts

    Spice up interiors while reducing noise with our tasteful range of acoustic metal, wood wool, and wood design ceiling materials, or choose floating solutions, such as canopies and vertical baffles.

    Station platforms

    Excellent for noise reduction and built to last, our acoustic metal ceiling systems are offered in a variety of standard and custom perforations to maximise tranquillity for passengers and staff at train station platforms.
