    Ceilings for Educational Buildings

    High-class ceilings to foster concentration

    Whether in schools or universities, ceilings for educational buildings should be designed studiously to optimise sight and sound. Our ceiling and wall system solutions ensure perfect acoustic balance in any space – from small classrooms to sweeping auditoriums, hushed libraries to humming gymnasiums. They also offer high light reflectance to keep students alert. With modular ceilings, floating ceilings, and wall panels in an array of colours, shapes, and sizes, you have endless possibilities to create stimulating spaces that nurture concentration and creativity.

    Benefits of Our Ceilings for for schools & universities
    Comfortable acoustics Balanced acoustic environments enhance occupant comfort, aid speech intelligibility, and support privacy. Our ceiling materials offer high sound absorption for clear communication and optimal sound attenuation to reduce distractions between rooms.
    High light reflectance Natural light has a positive effect on human mood and productivity. Returning up to 88% of light (versus 75% with standard ceilings), our ceiling tiles enhance occupant wellbeing and reduce the need for artificial lighting, thus also saving energy.
    Safe air quality High volatile organic compound (VOC) levels can impact occupant health. Most of our ceiling materials are certified by Eurofins Indoor Air Comfort® and Indoor Air Comfort® Gold to have low or very low VOC emissions.
    Classrooms

    Make sure students can hear clearly even at the back of the class with mineral, metal, wood wool, or wood design classroom ceiling tiles that enhance speech intelligibility. Their high light reflectance can also improve everyone’s moods and performance.

    Auditoriums

    Our mineral, metal, wood wool, and wood design auditorium ceilings fulfil a variety of acoustical needs, from sound reflection for controlled vibrancy to sound absorption for less reverberation. With a diverse choice of durable multi-material solutions, you can create the perfect stage for any performance.

    Libraries & Media centres

    Design the ideal space for quiet concentration with our unrivalled range of multi-material acoustic ceilings, and floating solutions. Their excellent light reflectance and reduced glare keeps students focused.

    Cafeterias

    Keep the joy but lose the buzz in school and university dining halls with ceiling tiles that absorb sound and prevent noise travelling to adjacent areas. Our durable, easy-to-clean solutions will sustain your great design.

    Gymnasiums

    The hard, reflective surfaces typical of gymnasiums can make the environment very noisy. Our ceiling tiles not only hush the racket but are also impact resistant to keep gyms looking fit for years.

    Corridors

    School and university corridors connect people and places. But the noise can be disorienting. Calm the crowd with acoustically optimised ceiling tiles and wall solutions that offer high sound absorption and attenuation.
